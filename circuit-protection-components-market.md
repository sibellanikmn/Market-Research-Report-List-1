Overcurrent protection components, such as fuses and circuit breakers, safeguard electronic systems by interrupting excessive current flow, preventing damage and fire risks. Meanwhile, overvoltage protection components, like surge protectors and voltage clamping devices, shield circuits from voltage spikes, which can harm sensitive electronics. Together, these components are essential for enhancing the reliability and safety of electrical systems across various applications.</p></p>
