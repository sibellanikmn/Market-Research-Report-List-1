<p><p>A stern drive propeller is a type of propulsion system commonly used in watercraft, combining features of both outboard and inboard engines. This system utilizes an engine mounted inside the boat, connected to a drive unit that extends through the transom and into the water. Stern drives are favored for their efficiency and ability to provide better maneuverability, making them particularly popular in recreational boating and commercial applications.</p><p>The Stern Drive Propeller Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.1% during the forecast period.
