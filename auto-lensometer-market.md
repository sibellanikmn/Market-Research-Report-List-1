<p><strong>The Auto Lensometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Visual Type Lensmeter</li><li>Projection Type Lensmeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Auto Lensometer market comprises two primary types: Visual Type Lensmeters and Projection Type Lensmeters. Visual Type Lensmeters use a manual visual assessment process, where the technician reads measurements directly through an eyepiece, offering straightforward functionality for basic prescriptions. In contrast, Projection Type Lensmeters project a light beam through the lens, automatically analyzing its refractive properties and providing digital readings. This automation enhances accuracy and efficiency, catering to varied optical retail and laboratory needs in the eyewear industry.</p></p>
