<p><p>Medical gas alarm panels are essential for monitoring and ensuring safe operation of medical gas systems. The area alarm panel serves localized zones, providing alerts for gas levels or leaks in specific areas. The master alarm panel centralizes monitoring for multiple zones, offering a comprehensive overview of gas statuses throughout a facility. In contrast, combination alarm panels integrate both area and master functionalities, allowing for efficient monitoring and quick response to potential issues across various locations in healthcare settings.</p></p>
