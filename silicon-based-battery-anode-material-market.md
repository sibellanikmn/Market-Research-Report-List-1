SiO/C refers to silicon oxide combined with carbon, offering enhanced stability and storage capacity, making it suitable for high-energy applications. Si/C, comprising silicon nanoparticles embedded within carbon matrices, enhances electrical conductivity and cycle life, addressing challenges of silicon expansion during charging cycles. Both types aim to improve the performance of lithium-ion batteries, catering to the growing demand for efficient energy storage solutions in various industries.</p></p>
