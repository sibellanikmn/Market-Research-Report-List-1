<p><strong>The Periodic Acid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Orthoperiodic Acid</li><li>Metaperiodic Acid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The periodic acid market is primarily divided into two types: orthoperiodic acid and metaperiodic acid. Orthoperiodic acid, often used in organic synthesis and analytical chemistry, possesses a stable, saturated structure, making it valuable in various applications, including histology and carbohydrate analysis. In contrast, metaperiodic acid, characterized by its less stable, unsaturated structure, finds its utility in dehydrogenation processes and as an oxidizing agent in chemical reactions. Both types cater to different industrial needs and research fields.</p></p>
