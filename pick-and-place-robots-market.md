<p><p>The pick and place robots market comprises several types, including articulated, delta, SCARA, and Cartesian robots. Articulated robots feature rotary joints, allowing for complex movements and versatility. Delta robots excel in high-speed applications with their lightweight, parallel-arm design. SCARA robots are ideal for horizontal movements, combining speed and precision, suitable for assembly tasks. Cartesian robots use linear axes for simple, straight-line motion, making them cost-effective for tasks like packaging and palletizing. Each type serves distinct industrial automation needs.</p></p>
