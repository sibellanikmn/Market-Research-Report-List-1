<p><p>The hydrocarbon solvents market is primarily categorized into two types: aliphatic and aromatic. Aliphatic solvents, derived from linear or branched hydrocarbons, are typically used in applications like paint thinners and cleaners due to their low toxicity and volatility. In contrast, aromatic solvents, which contain benzene rings, are valued for their solvent power and stability, making them suitable for industrial applications like coatings and adhesives. Both types serve distinct roles in various sectors, including automotive, construction, and manufacturing.</p></p>
