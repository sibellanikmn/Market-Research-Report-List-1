<p><ul><li>Low Frequency</li><li>High Frequency</li><li>UHF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RFID electronic ear tags are categorized into three main frequency types: Low Frequency (LF), High Frequency (HF), and Ultra-High Frequency (UHF). LF tags operate typically between 125-134 kHz, suitable for proximity applications like animal tracking due to their resistance to interference. HF tags, operating at 13.56 MHz, enable data transfer over short distances and are often used for product identification. UHF tags, ranging from 300 MHz to 960 MHz, offer longer read distances and faster data transfer, making them ideal for inventory management and logistics.</p></p>
