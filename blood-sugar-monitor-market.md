<p><strong>The Blood Sugar Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Glucose Oxidase</li><li>Glucose Dehydrogenase</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The blood sugar monitor market is categorized primarily into three types based on the enzymatic methods used for glucose detection. Glucose oxidase monitors utilize the enzyme glucose oxidase to oxidize glucose, producing hydrogen peroxide, which is measured for glucose concentration. Glucose dehydrogenase monitors employ glucose dehydrogenase, facilitating the conversion of glucose into gluconolactone and yielding electrons for measurement. Other market types may include various non-enzymatic methods or innovative technologies that offer alternative means for blood glucose monitoring.</p></p>
