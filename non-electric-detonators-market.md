<p><p>The non-electric detonators market is primarily divided into two types: delay detonators and non-delayed detonators. Delay detonators are designed to provide a timed ignition sequence, allowing for better control over explosions and enhancing safety in operations such as mining and construction. Non-delayed detonators, on the other hand, initiate an explosion immediately upon activation, often used in scenarios requiring instantaneous results. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, ensuring efficient and effective detonation processes.</p></p>
