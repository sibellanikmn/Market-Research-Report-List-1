<p><p>The Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure (BiPAP) ventilators market comprises two primary types: Fixed Pressure BiPAP devices and Auto Adjusting BiPAP devices. Fixed Pressure BiPAP devices deliver a constant pressure for inhalation and exhalation, providing consistent respiratory support. In contrast, Auto Adjusting BiPAP devices automatically adjust the pressure levels based on the patient's breathing patterns, ensuring optimal support during varying conditions. Both types cater to patients with respiratory conditions, enhancing their comfort and effectiveness in treatment.</p></p>
