<p><strong>The Server Power Supply Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ATX Power</li><li>SSI Power</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The server power supply market can be categorized into two main types: ATX Power and SSI Power. ATX Power supplies are widely used in standard servers, offering versatility and compatibility for various components. They typically support a range of voltages and have modular designs for easy upgrades. On the other hand, SSI Power supplies follow the Server System Infrastructure standards, ensuring a higher level of reliability and efficiency for enterprise-level servers. SSI PSUs are designed for optimized thermal management and power delivery in data centers.</p></p>
