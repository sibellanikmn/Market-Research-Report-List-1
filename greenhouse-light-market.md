<p><p>The greenhouse light market consists of various technologies, primarily LED, sodium lamps, and halogen lamps. LED lights are energy-efficient, long-lasting, and customizable, making them ideal for precise growth needs. Sodium lamps, particularly high-pressure sodium (HPS) bulbs, are favored for their intense light output and efficiency in flowering stages. Halogen lamps, while less common, provide high-intensity lighting with a warm spectrum but consume more energy. Each type serves distinct agricultural purposes, balancing efficiency, light quality, and operational costs.</p></p>
