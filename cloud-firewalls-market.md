<p><p>The cloud firewall market consists of various types, primarily including Software as a Service (SaaS) firewalls and Next Generation Firewalls (NGFW). SaaS firewalls provide security through a cloud-based service, allowing easy management and scalability without the need for on-premises hardware. In contrast, Next Generation Firewalls combine traditional firewall capabilities with advanced features like intrusion detection, application awareness, and deep packet inspection, offering enhanced protection against sophisticated threats. Both types cater to organizations seeking robust security solutions for their cloud environments.</p></p>
