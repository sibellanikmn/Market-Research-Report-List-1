<p><ul><li>Mono-Si Modules</li><li>Multi-Si Modules</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The crystalline silicon photovoltaic (PV) cells market is primarily divided into two types: monocrystalline silicon (Mono-Si) modules and polycrystalline silicon (Multi-Si) modules. Mono-Si modules are made from a single crystal structure, offering higher efficiency and space-saving advantages, making them ideal for residential and commercial applications. Conversely, Multi-Si modules, composed of multiple silicon crystals, are generally more affordable and easier to produce but have lower efficiency. Both types are widely used in solar energy systems, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
