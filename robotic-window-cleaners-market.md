<p><strong>The Robotic Window Cleaners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vacuum Suction</li><li>Fan Adsorption</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The robotic window cleaners market can be categorized into two main types: vacuum suction and fan adsorption. Vacuum suction cleaners use powerful motors to create suction that adheres to the glass surface, efficiently removing dirt and grime. In contrast, fan adsorption models employ airflow generated by fans to cling to windows, allowing them to glide smoothly while cleaning. Both technologies provide innovative solutions for window maintenance, each catering to specific customer preferences and cleaning requirements, enhancing convenience and efficiency in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
