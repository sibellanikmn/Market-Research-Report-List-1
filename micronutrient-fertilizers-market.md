<p><p>The micronutrient fertilizers market comprises various types, including iron (Fe) fertilizers, manganese (Mn) fertilizers, zinc (Zn) fertilizers, copper (Cu) fertilizers, and combined (combi) fertilizers. Fe fertilizers enhance plant growth by preventing chlorosis and improving photosynthesis. Mn fertilizers aid in enzyme function and photosynthesis. Zn fertilizers are critical for enzyme activity and plant development. Cu fertilizers support photosynthesis and reproductive growth. Combi fertilizers offer a balanced mix of these nutrients, catering to diverse crop needs and enhancing overall agricultural productivity.</p></p>
