<p><strong>The Radar Reflectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Octahedral Corner Reflector</li><li>Luneberg Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radar reflectors market primarily features two types: octahedral corner reflectors and Luneberg lenses. Octahedral corner reflectors consist of three mutually perpendicular surfaces that reflect radar signals back toward the source, making them highly effective for various applications, including marine navigation and aeronautics. Luneberg lenses, on the other hand, are spherical dielectric devices that enable wide-angle radar reflection, enhancing signal coverage and detection accuracy. Both types play critical roles in improving radar system performance across sectors such as defense, aviation, and maritime safety.</p></p>
