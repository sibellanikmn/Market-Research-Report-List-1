<p><p>The air atomizing nozzles market features three main types: flat spray nozzles, full cone nozzles, and hollow cone nozzles. Flat spray nozzles produce a wide, flat spray pattern ideal for coating or surface treatment applications. Full cone nozzles generate a uniform circular spray, suitable for cooling or washing applications. Hollow cone nozzles create a ring-shaped spray for efficient mixing or atomization in processes like fuel injection. Each type offers distinct advantages based on specific operational requirements.</p></p>
