<p><strong>The Polyester Non-woven Fabric Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spunbond</li><li>Needle Punch</li><li>Stitch Bond</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Polyester Non-woven Fabric market encompasses three key types: Spunbond, Needle Punch, and Stitch Bond. Spunbond fabrics are produced through a process that involves extruding polymer filaments, resulting in lightweight and durable materials used in hygiene products and geotextiles. Needle Punch fabrics are created by mechanically entangling fibers, providing excellent strength and thickness suitable for automotive and upholstery applications. Stitch Bond fabrics combine knitting and bonding techniques, offering flexibility and stability, making them ideal for applications in medical and industrial sectors.</p></p>
