<p><strong>The Nitinol based Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Permanent IVC Filters</li><li>Retrievable IVC Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nitinol-based filters are primarily used in the treatment of venous thromboembolism. The market is divided into two main types: permanent IVC filters and retrievable IVC filters. Permanent IVC filters are designed for long-term implantation to prevent pulmonary embolism by trapping emboli indefinitely. In contrast, retrievable IVC filters are intended for temporary placement, allowing for removal once the risk of thromboembolism decreases. The choice between these filters depends on patient-specific conditions and treatment strategies, influencing market dynamics.</p></p>
