<p><strong>The Desktop Spectrometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Absorption Spectrometer</li><li>Optical Emission Spectrometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The desktop spectrometer market is primarily categorized into two types: optical absorption spectrometers and optical emission spectrometers. Optical absorption spectrometers analyze the wavelengths absorbed by a sample, providing insights into its composition and concentration. In contrast, optical emission spectrometers measure the light emitted from a sample when it is excited, offering information about its elemental structure. Both types are essential tools in various fields, including environmental monitoring, materials science, and pharmaceuticals, facilitating qualitative and quantitative analysis in a compact form.</p></p>
