<p><p>The cutter staplers market is primarily segmented into two types: endo staplers and open staplers. Endo staplers are designed for minimally invasive surgical procedures, allowing surgeons to perform operations through small incisions, enhancing patient recovery time and reducing complications. In contrast, open staplers are used in traditional open surgeries, providing reliable tissue closure and secure suturing in larger surgical fields. Both types are essential in various surgical disciplines, catering to different procedural needs and patient factors.</p></p>
