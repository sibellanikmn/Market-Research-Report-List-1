Negative steering, on the other hand, refers to the drill deviating from its intended path due to external factors or limitations, requiring adjustments to correct the trajectory. A market with no positive or negative steering indicates stability, where drills follow predetermined patterns without significant deviation, ensuring reliable and predictable drilling outcomes.</p></p>
