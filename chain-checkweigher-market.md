<p><strong>The Chain Checkweigher Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-Motion Checkweighers</li><li>Intermittent Checkweighers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chain checkweigher market features two primary types: in-motion checkweighers and intermittent checkweighers. In-motion checkweighers continuously weigh products as they move along a production line, ensuring real-time accuracy and efficiency for high-speed operations. In contrast, intermittent checkweighers pause the product during weighing, providing precise measurement but typically operating at lower speeds. Both types serve to enhance quality control and ensure compliance with regulatory standards by accurately assessing product weight, thus minimizing waste and optimizing production processes.</p></p>
