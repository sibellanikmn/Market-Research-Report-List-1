<p><p>The semiconductor stocker market is categorized into semi-automatic and fully-automatic systems. Semi-automatic stockers require some manual intervention for loading and unloading substrates, providing a balance between efficiency and operator control. In contrast, fully-automatic stockers operate independently, using advanced robotics and automated systems to handle substrates without human intervention, maximizing throughput and minimizing errors. Both types are essential in semiconductor manufacturing, with varying levels of automation catering to different production needs and operational preferences.</p></p>
