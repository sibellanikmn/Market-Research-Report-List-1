<p><p>Polyamide 612 (PA612) is a bio-based, semi-crystalline polymer known for its excellent abrasion resistance and thermal stability. In the market, it is classified into three types based on molecular arrangement: isotactic, atactic, and syndiotactic. Isotactic PA612 features a regular arrangement, offering high crystallinity and strength. Atactic PA612, with a random configuration, is less crystalline and flexible. Syndiotactic PA612 presents an alternating arrangement, balancing properties between isotactic and atactic forms, enhancing its suitability for diverse applications in textiles, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
