<p><p>The Renewable Natural Gas (RNG) market comprises several resource types. Agricultural RNG is derived from organic waste, such as manure, through anaerobic digestion, transforming it into a clean energy source. Sewage and wastewater RNG is produced from the anaerobic treatment of municipal sewage, capturing methane during the decomposition process. Landfill RNG, on the other hand, is extracted from landfills where organic materials decompose, releasing biogas that can be refined into RNG. Each resource offers sustainable energy solutions, contributing to reduced greenhouse gas emissions.</p></p>
