<p><p>The piezoceramic components market is primarily categorized into PZT-based, PMN-based, and other types. PZT (Lead Zirconate Titanate) is widely used for its robust properties, making it ideal for applications in sensors and actuators. PMN (Lead Magnesium Niobate) offers superior piezoelectric performance, especially in high-frequency applications but is more expensive. Other materials may include various compositions and composite structures, catering to niche applications in industries like aerospace, medical devices, and consumer electronics, each driven by specific performance needs.</p></p>
