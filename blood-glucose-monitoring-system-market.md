<p><p>The blood glucose monitoring system market is primarily divided into two types: Self-Monitoring Blood Glucose (SMBG) devices and Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM) devices. SMBG devices require users to manually check their blood glucose levels using portable meters and test strips. In contrast, CGM devices offer real-time glucose monitoring through a sensor worn on the body, providing continuous data and alerts for fluctuations. Both systems are vital for diabetes management, enabling patients to make informed decisions about their health.</p></p>
