<p><p>The Long Range Obstacle Detection System market encompasses three primary technologies: radar, laser scanners, and sonar. Radar utilizes radio waves to detect and track obstacles over long distances, making it effective in diverse weather conditions. Laser scanners employ light pulses for high-precision measurements, ideal for detailed mapping in various environments. Sonar relies on sound waves to detect objects underwater or in challenging terrains, offering unique advantages in marine and industrial applications. Each technology serves specific needs in obstacle detection and navigation.</p></p>
