<p><ul><li>Static Straight Water Screen System</li><li>Static Bow Water Screen System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Static water screen systems are used to filter debris from water in various applications, such as power plants and irrigation systems. The static straight water screen system features vertical screens that capture debris while allowing water to flow through, ideal for low-velocity environments. In contrast, the static bow water screen system includes curved screens that improve flow efficiency and debris capture, making it suitable for higher-velocity conditions. Both systems enhance water quality and protect downstream equipment from damage.</p></p>
