<p><strong>The Carck Meters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Crack Meters</li><li>Wire Crack Meters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Crack meters are specialized instruments used to monitor the stability of structures by measuring crack movements. The mechanical crack meters utilize a series of gears and levers to provide precise readings of displacement, making them reliable for long-term monitoring. In contrast, wire crack meters utilize a tensioned wire system that measures changes in crack width by detecting wire movement. Both types are essential for assessing structural integrity in construction and geotechnical applications, ensuring safety and performance over time.</p></p>
