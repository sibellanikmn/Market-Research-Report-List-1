<p><strong>The Label Press Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Digital</li><li>Analog</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Label press markets can be classified into two main types: digital and analog. Digital label presses utilize digital printing technologies, allowing for quick production runs, customization, and on-demand printing without the need for traditional printing plates. They are favored for their efficiency and flexibility in design changes. In contrast, analog label presses, like flexographic and gravure printing, rely on physical plates and are typically used for high-volume production. They offer excellent print quality and color consistency but involve longer setup times.</p></p>
