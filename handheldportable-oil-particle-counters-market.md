<p><p>The handheld/portable oil particle counters market features different types based on the number of measurement channels. Single channel particle counters measure particles in one size range, providing basic analysis. Two channel particle counters enhance detection by analyzing two separate size ranges, allowing for a more detailed evaluation of oil contamination. Multi-channel particle counters offer the most comprehensive solution, measuring multiple size ranges simultaneously, enabling thorough monitoring of oil quality and contamination levels for various applications, including industrial and laboratory settings.</p></p>
